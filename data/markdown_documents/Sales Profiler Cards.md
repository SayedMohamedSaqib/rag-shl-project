# Assessment: Sales Profiler Cards

## Description
The SHL Sales Model is mapped to the SHL Universal Competency Model (UCF). The Sales Model Profiler cards have been developed to enable you to use the Sales Report more effectively, by helping you to understand which competencies and motivators described in the report are most important to a specific sales role.
